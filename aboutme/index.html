About me

Hello, my name is Chris, also known as TwentiesFour. I am 22 years old and come from Heidelberg. Video games have been with me since my childhood. My first experiences were on the Game Boy Advance SP, where I tried to catch little creatures with a ball. Later, I switched to the computer and played classics like Age of Empires, Stronghold, and Need for Speed. I also owned a Playstation 3 and Playstation 4 before eventually returning to the computer, where I mainly play to this day. For local game nights with friends or family, I bought a Nintendo Switch. My current favorite game is Overwatch 2.

Currently, I am investing a lot of time and effort in expanding my programming skills and deepening my understanding of various programming languages and technologies. I am not only working on the development of letszock.de, but also on other exciting projects that focus on various areas such as web development, app development, and data analysis.

At the beginning of the year, I started generating images using AI to simplify and improve the creative design of visual content. My experiments with different algorithms and network architectures have led me to delve deeper into the application of machine learning in image generation. I find it fascinating how AI is able to recognize patterns in the data and create complex images that almost look like they were made by a human.
