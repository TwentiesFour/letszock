About me

Hey, I'm Chris, 22 years old and I'm from Heidelberg, Germany. Video games have been with me for at least half my life. It all started with the Game Boy Advance SP, where I caught little funny creatures with a ball. It continued on the computer with classic games like Age of Empires, Stronghold and Need for Speed. After that, I played on Playstation 3 and Playstation 4 until I returned to the computer at some point, where I still mainly play games. At some point, I bought a Nintendo Switch for local games with family or friends. My current favorite game is Overwatch 2.

I started programming on January 14, 2023. I started my first project with my website letszock.com. If you don't want to miss anything more about this and other projects, then have a look at my social media.
